Privacy Policy

This application uses the TikTok API to publish content on behalf of authenticated users.

Data collected:

  • TikTok account identifiers
  • OAuth access and refresh tokens

Data usage:

  • Used solely to authenticate users and publish content they authorize
  • Not shared with third parties

Data storage:

  • Stored securely within the n8n automation platform

Users may revoke access at any time through their TikTok account settings.
